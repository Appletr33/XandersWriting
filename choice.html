    The Fungi Funk Sundae Silence In Questioning

In the deep woods, where the trees stood tall,
I killed another, heard the last breath fall.
Blood on my hands, a heavy toll to bear,
As if fate decreed, we kill each other in despair.



Like I'm told, it's part of the war,
A deadly game, no one can ignore.
A bullet blazes by, grazes a tree,
I feel the chill, nearly died, just like me.



In the gunfight scene, I rack my gun,
Trembling fingers, what have I become?
A tank shoots, the ground trembles beneath,
Fear grips my soul, I try to breathe.



A drone strike hits the tank, flames everywhere,
A burning metal trap, no escape, no prayer.
In the gunfight scene, another soldier fell,
I close my eyes, hear the tolling of a knell.



I get shot in the thigh, pain engulfs me,
Drones above drop mortar shell, no mercy to see.
My shattered heart scene, tears streaming down,
As I realize, I'll never make it back to my hometown.



cut.
